Introduction

Wel­come to Mira­cia /miɹat­sia/, a cam­paign set­ting for Dun­geons & Drag­ons 5th Edi­tion fol­low­ing the fall­out of a recent indus­trial rev­o­lu­tion on a mag­i­cal world.

Mira­cia is a vast region on the planet Hun­yil /ˈhwɪnyil/, a gas dwarf filled with vast con­ti­nents sus­pended in seas of air. Air­ships sail the open skies, fueled with the magic fuel aure mined from the lower isles of Mira­cia. But the rapid pace of advance­ment has its price—aure has its price, and the veins are run­ning dry. Isles drained of their aure lose their buoy­ancy and sink into the cloud sea, tak­ing their inhab­i­tants with them to suf­fo­cate in the depths. Who will soar, and who will sink?
